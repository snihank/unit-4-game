Crystals Collector

A random number is displayed in the green box.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a cystal, you will add a specific number of points to your total score.

You win by matching your total score to that random number, you lose if your total score goes beyond the random number in the green box.

The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal and generates a new random number in the green box.
